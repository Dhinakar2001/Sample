Brahmastra: Part One – Shiva (pronounced [b???ma?st??]) is a 2022 Indian Hindi-language fantasy action-adventure film written and directed by Ayan Mukerji. It is produced by Karan Johar, Apoorva Mehta, Namit Malhotra, and Mukerji – in his debut production – under the production companies Dharma Productions, Starlight Pictures, and Prime Focus in association with Star Studios, along with Ranbir Kapoor and Marijke DeSouza. It stars Amitabh Bachchan, Kapoor, Alia Bhatt, Mouni Roy, and Nagarjuna Akkineni. The film is intended to serve as the first instalment of a planned trilogy, which is itself planned to be part of an extended cinematic universe titled Astraverse.[8]

Brahmastra was announced in July 2014[9] but was delayed by several years. Principal photography lasted for five years from February 2018 to March 2022,[10] with filming locations including Bulgaria, London, New York, Edinburgh, Thailand, Manali, Mumbai and Varanasi.[11] Production and release of the film was delayed multiple times due to the COVID-19 pandemic.[12]
